Counting Battle!

Counting Battle! is a multiplayer Go territory counting game based on Anton Tobi's Count!

With this version of the game, you can practice counting territory while competing with other players in real-time.

How to Play

Goal: For each position, determine which side (Black or White) controls more territory. All positions assume no komi and no prisoners, with equal stones for both sides and no sekis.
