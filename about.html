Counting Battle!

This is a Go territory counting game based on antonTobi's Count!

On this version of the game, you can practice counting territory while competing with other players in real-time.

There's also a hard mode where you can practice exact territory counting instead of just choosing the color that won.

After the game, you can also review each position with the game summary view.

Game Setup:

For the given position, determine which side (Black or White) controls more territory. All positions assume no komi, no prisoners, and no sekis.
