Counting Battle!

This is a Go territory counting game based on antonTobi's Count!

On this version of the game, you can practice counting territory while competing with other players in real-time.

There's also a hard mode where you can practice exact territory counting instead of just choosing the right color.

Game Setup:

For each position, determine which side (Black or White) controls more territory. All positions assume no komi and no prisoners, with equal stones for both sides and no sekis.
